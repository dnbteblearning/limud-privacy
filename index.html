Privacy Policy

Chazarah Tracker — Last updated: February 4, 2026

In short: Chazarah Tracker does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. Everything stays on your device.

Chazarah Tracker is a fully offline application. We do not collect any personal information or usage data. The app does not connect to the internet for any purpose.

Data Storage

All app data — including your learning progress, review schedules, tracks, and settings — is stored locally on your device using an on-device database. This data never leaves your device.

Third-Party Services

The app does not use any third-party services, analytics tools, advertising networks, or tracking technologies.

Permissions

The app may request permission to send local notifications to remind you of upcoming reviews. No data is sent externally as part of this feature.

Children's Privacy

Because the app collects no data whatsoever, it is safe for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.
